Wang Yibo

WANG YIBO is a Chinese actor, singer, rapper, dancer, model, tv show host, professional motorcycle racer and a member of Korean-Chinese boy group UNIQ.

Learn More

Who is Wang Yibo?

Wang Yibo was born August 5th, 1997 in Luoyang, China.

He started dancing when he young and competed in the "I'm the Best Dancer" Competition in 2011 where he placed in the top sixteen of the hip-hop category.

Shortly after, he signed to Yuehua Entertainment where he debuted in the boy group UNIQ in 2014 as the main dancer and lead rapper with the song "Falling in Love."

He has released multiple solo singles such as Fire (2019), Lucky (2019) and Wu Gan (2020).

"I've never thought of wanting to reach the peak. I just want to do what I love."

More UNIQ

Yibo received increased popularity and recognition for his role of Lan Wangji in The Untamed (2019).

The Untamed is a Chinese drama adapted from the xianxia novel Mo Dao Zu Shi by Mo Xiang Tong Xiu.

It follows the adventures of two soulmate cultivators who travel to solve a series of mysteries that link to a tragic event in the past (1).

Yibo has also been a host for the Chinese variety show Day Day Up since 2016.

Recently, he has been captain on the Chinese dance competition "Street Dance of China" and participated in the 2020 Zhuhai International Circuit on the Yamaha Man Lee Tat Racing Team.
